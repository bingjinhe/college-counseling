Background

I graduated from Tsinghua University, Beijing China with a CS and Economics dual degree. After getting a CS master degree in the US, I started a career in the technology industry, and worked in companies such as Cisco, Amazon, and a few startups. I moved to Lexington, MA, when my kids attended middle schools there. Even though both my kids went to private high schools, I am pretty familiar with students' growth path and available resources in public schools as well.

I have two boys. My older one is the 2013 Mathcounts national champion. He graduated from Phillips Exeter Academy in 2017, and is one of the few high school students who went to three US Olympiad Camps (Math, Physics and USACO)。 He also did math and physics research outside of school, which won awards like Siemens Competition and Regeneron Science Talent Search finalist. He is one of the few students who is heavily involved in both STEM competitions and research. He graduated from Harvard in 2021 with a math bachelor and CS master double degree. My younger one is passionate about CS and Entrepreneurship (founder of HackNEHS). He figured this out after many other unsuccessful attempts, such as various math and science competitions. These experience was very frustrating, but was an unavoidable journey of self-exploration. It is good he finally found his true passion. He graduated from Phillips Andover in 2018, and graduated from UPenn in 2022 with double degress from Engineering College and Wharton Business School(M&T program), and a master degree in data stats.

During those years overseeing my two boys and other students, I accumulated a rich inventory of resources in high school competitions (math, physics, chemistry, biology, cs, writing, economics) and research (outside school and over the summer), as well as high quality program choices in business and entrepreneurship. In recent years, I also advised a few students interested in writing, debate and politics. Every student is unique, there is no way to clone any other person's activities. Blindly copying the growth path of other high achievers will only lead to more nervousness and frustration. Parents should encourage their kids to keep exploring one's own interests and potential, have a realistic realization of your capability and strength, and create a unique brand which belongs to yourself. Gradually learn the skills of critical thinking and decision making. Even though the planning is done to prepare and get ready for college applications, the ultimate goal is to train students into an independent, confident and impactful self. The two goals do not conflict.

I do not pick on how good grades a student currently has, but I pick on student attitude -- I am only willing to work with self-disciplined students and reasonable/realistic parents. I am confident I can help students challenge themselves as long as we both work together with trust and strategies.

Middle School Service

1. 8th grade students looking to apply for top 10 private high schools in United States or 7th grade students who are applying for CDB Scholarship

College Application

My package includes the following items:
1. Recommend applying to a couple quality summer programs after 11th grade, try to correlate the program with student interest and college major. Help with application materials and application essays.
2. Recommend a list of colleges to apply based on students interests and strength. Around 15, vary in difficulties.
3 Make a school visiting plan, and application milestones for the summer.
4. Review Common App applications, including 5 awards, 10 activities, essays, optional uploads, showing a clear persona in the overall portfolio (UC schools, MIT, Georgetown don't use Common App, Oxford/Cambridge has its own system too. They are included if students choose to apply.)
5. Brainstorming essays topics for all schools, maximized topic re-use
6. Assigned assay tutor will be responsible for polishing up to 10 school essays, UC system counts as one. (based on past experience, students can easily recycle essays for rest of schools beyond the 10th one). Essay is revised many times, until the tutor, student and me are all happy. Important essays will be reviewed by more than one tutor.
7. Advise which teacher to use for recommendation letters and strategies to ask for a quality letter.
8 Answer all questions from students and parents during the application cycle. Turn around time is usually less than 24 hours.
9 Advise how to pick optional materials, such as resume, video upload, research abstract, etc.
10 Help students to talk to other students how are already in college to get insider information for a particular school.
11 Prepare for interview content and rehearsal
12 Advise off waiting list actions

My team has the following strength:
1 文书老师非常给力，是我这几年不断筛选出来的好老师。给的建议非常具体，不会让学生觉得很迷茫，不知道怎样继续。每年都有八月份了来找我改文书的，说他们用的中介文书改不下去了。
2 推荐信上我下很多功夫帮学生，大部分中介不管这一项，而这是一个非常重要环节。我有一套自己的方法让学生影响老师写推荐信的内容，效果非常好。但是要早做准备。
